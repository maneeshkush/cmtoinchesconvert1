一千焦耳等於多少焦耳？

焦耳和千焦是用於測量能量的國際單位制 (SI) 的單位。焦耳的標準符號是 J[1]，而千焦耳的符號是 KJ。1 J 正好等於 0.001 KJ，因此 1 千焦耳等於 1,000 焦耳。

焦耳和千焦耳的區別

Joules and kilojoules are used to measure energy, but differ in magnitude. While the kilojoule is a derivative of the joule, the joule itself is a derived metric, which is equal to the energy transferred to an object when a force of one Newton acts on it in the direction of its motion through a distance of one meter ( 1 Newton Meter or N⋅m). A similar definition is the energy dissipated as heat by an electric current of one ampere passing for one second through a resistance of one ohm.

The energy unit is named after the 19th-century English physicist James Prescott Joule (1818–1889), who had significant contributions to physics, particularly relating to the nature of heat, and the discovery of its relationship to mechanical work. is given credit for.
