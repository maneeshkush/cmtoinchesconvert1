Weighted grade calculation

The weighted grade is equal to the sum of the product of the weights (w) in percent (%) times the grade (g):

Weighted grade =  w1×g1+ w2×g2+ w3×g3+...

Example 1

Math course with grade of 77 and weight of 30%.

Biology course with grade of 85 and weight of 50%.

History course with grade of 73 and weight of 20%.

The weighted average grade is calculated by:

Weighted grade =

 =  w1×g1+ w2×g2+ w3×g3

 = (30×77+50×85+20×73) = 80.20

Example 2

Math course with grade of 79 and weight of 30%.

Biology course with grade of 89 and weight of 50%.

History course with grade of 76 and weight of 20%.

The weighted average grade is calculated by:

Weighted grade =

 =  w1×g1+ w2×g2+ w3×g3

 = (30×79+50×89+20×76) = 83.40

 

When the weights are not in percent (hours or points...), you should also divided by the sum of the weights:

Weighted grade = (w1×g1+ w2×g2+ w3×g3+...) / (w1+w2+w3+...)

Example 1

3 points Math course with grade of 77.

5 points Biology course with grade of 85.

2 points History course with grade of 73.

The weighted average grade is calculated by:

Weighted grade =

 = (w1×g1+ w2×g2+ w3×g3) / (w1+w2+w3)

 = (3×77+ 5×85+ 2×73) / (3+5+2) = 80.20

Example 2

3 points Math course with grade of 79.

5 points Biology course with grade of 79.

2 points History course with grade of 76.

The weighted average grade is calculated by:

Weighted grade =

 = (w1×g1+ w2×g2+ w3×g3) / (w1+w2+w3)

 = (3×79+ 5×89+ 2×76) / (3+5+2) = 83.40
