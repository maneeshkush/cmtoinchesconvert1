Percentage of a value calculation

What is 20% of $50?

20% is mutiplied by $50:

20% × $50 = (20/100) × $50 = 0.2 × $50 = $10

Percentage calculation

$12 is what percent of $50?

$12 is divided by $50 and multiplied by 100%:

($12 / $50) × 100% = 24%

Whole value calculation

$15 is 20% of what?

$15 is divided by 20%:

$15 / 20% = ($15 / 20) × 100 = $75

Percentage change calculation

What is the percentage change from $40 to $70?

The difference between $70 and $40 is divided by $40 and multiplied by 100%:

[($70 - $40) / $40] × 100% = 0.25 × 100% = 75%
