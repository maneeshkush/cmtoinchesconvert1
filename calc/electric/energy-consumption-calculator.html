Energy consumption calculation

So The energy E in kilowatt-hours (kWh) per day is equal to the power P in watts (W) times number of usage hours per day t divided by 1000 watts per kilowatt.

E(kWh/day) = P(W) × t(h/day) / 1000(W/kW)
