KW to kilowatt-hours calculation

So The energy E in kilowatt-hours (kWh) is equal to the power P in kilowatts (kW), times the time period t in hours (h).

E(kWh) = P(kW) × t(h)

Example 1

What is the energy consumption in watt-hour when the power consumption is 3 kilowatts for time duration of 3 hours?

E = 3kW × 3h = 9 kWh

Example 2

What is the energy consumption in watt-hour when the power consumption is 5 kilowatts for time duration of 4 hours?

E = 5kW × 4h = 20 kWh

Example 3

What is the energy consumption in watt-hour when the power consumption is 5 kilowatts for time duration of 6 hours?

E = 5kW × 6h = 30 kWh

Example 4

What is the energy consumption in watt-hour when the power consumption is 5 kilowatts for time duration of 10 hours?

E = 5kW × 10h = 50 kWh
