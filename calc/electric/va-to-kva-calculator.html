Volt-amps to kVA calculation formula

So The apparent power S in kilovolt-amps (kVA) is equal to the apparent power S in volt-amps (VA), divided by [1000].

S(kVA) =  S(VA) / 1000

Example 1

What is the apparent power in kilovolt-amps when the apparent power in volt-amps is 2000VA?

Solution:

S = 2000VA / 1000 = 2kVA

Example 2

What is the apparent power in kilovolt-amps when the apparent power in volt-amps is 4000VA?

Solution:

S = 5000VA / 1000 = 5kVA

Example 3

What is the apparent power in kilovolt-amps when the apparent power in volt-amps is 10000VA?

Solution:

S = 10000VA / 1000 = 13kVA

Example 4

Solution:

S = 13000VA / 1000 = 13kVA
