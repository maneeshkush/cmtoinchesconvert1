How to convert meters to inches

1 meter is equal to 39.37007874 inches:

1m = (1/0.0254)″ = 39.37007874″

So distance d in inches (″) is equal to the distance d in meters (m) divided by [0.0254].

d(″) =  d(m) / 0.0254

Convert 0.3 meters to inches:

d(″) = 0.3m / 0.0254 = 11.811″
