How to convert kilometers to miles

1 kilometer is equal to 1/1.609344 miles:

1m = 1/1.609344mi = 0.62137119224mi

2 kilometer is equal to 2/1.609344 miles:

2m = 2/1.609344mi = 1.2427423845mi

So distance d in miles (mi) is equal to the distance d in kilometers (km) divided by [1.609344].

d(mi) =  d(km) / 1.609344
