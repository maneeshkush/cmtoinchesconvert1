How to convert feet to millimeters

1 foot is equal to 304.8 millimeters:

1 ft = 304.8 mm

2 foot is equal to 609.6 millimeters:

2 ft = 609.6 mm

So distance d in millimeters (mm) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times [304.8]

d(mm) =  d(ft) × 304.8

Convert 3 feet to millimeters:
