How to convert BTU to kWh

How to convert energy in BTU to kilowatt-hours (kWh).

How to convert BTU to kWh

How to convert BTU (IT) to kilowatt-hour:

1 BTUIT = 0.00029307107017 kWh

 

So the energy in kilowatt-hours E(kWh) is equal to 0.00029307107017 times the energy in BTUs E(BTU):

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × E(BTU)

 

Example

Convert 3000 BTU to kWh:

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × 3000 BTU = 0.879 kWh

Let's first summarize what BTU and kWh are:

BTU stands for British Thermal Unit. It is a unit of heat (cooling) and is defined as the amount of heat required to heat (or cool) 1 pound of water by one degree (°F).
kWh stands for kilowatt hour. It is a unit of electrical energy and is defined as the amount of electricity used to run a 1,000W (1kW) device for 1 hour.
How to convert BTU to kWh?

 

You only need to follow this conversion rule:

1 BTU is equal to 0.00029308323 kWh. This means that 3,412 BTU is equal to 1 kWh.

To calculate BTU to kWh, you simply multiply BTU by 0.00029308323. This is a fairly small number and you would have to type a lot to use a hand calculator to convert BTU to kWh.
