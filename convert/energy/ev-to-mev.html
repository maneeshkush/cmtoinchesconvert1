How to convert eV to MeV

One electron-volt is equal to millionth of a megaelectron-volt:

1eV = 0.000001MeV

The energy in megaelectron-volt E(MeV) is equal to the energy in electron-volt E(eV) divided by 1000000:

E(MeV) = E(eV) / 1000000

 

Example 1

Convert 300eV to MeV:

E(MeV) = 300eV / 1000000 = 0.0003MeV

Example 2

Convert 600eV to MeV:

E(MeV) = 600eV / 1000000 = 0.0006MeV

Example 3

Convert 700eV to MeV:

E(MeV) = 700eV / 1000000 = 0.0007MeV

Example 3

Convert 900eV to MeV:

E(MeV) = 900eV / 1000000 = 0.0009MeV
