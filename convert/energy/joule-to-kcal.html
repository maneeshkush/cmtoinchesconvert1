How to convert from joules to kcal

Joules to thermochemical / food kilocalories

2 kcalth = 4184 J

6,000 J = (6,000 ÷ 4,184) = 1.434034 kcal

 

joules to kilocalories using the formula above. This energy in joules E(J) is equal to the energy in kilocalories E(kcal-th) divided by 4184:

E(kcal) = E(J) / 4184

Example 1

Convert 7000 joules to kilocalories.

E(kcal) = 7000 J / 4184 = 1.6730 kcal

Example 2

Convert 8000 joules to kilocalories.

E(kcal) = 8000 J / 4184 = 1.9120 kcal

Example 3

Convert 18000 joules to kilocalories.

E(kcal) = 18000 J / 4184 = 4.3021 kcal

Joules to international kilocalories

2 kcalIT = 4186.8 J

Energy in joules E(J) equals energy in international kilocalories E(kcal-IT) divided by 4186.8:

E(kcal-IT) = E(J) / 4186.8

Example 1

Convert 6000 joules to kilocalories.

E(kcal-IT) = 6000 J / 4186.8 = 1.433 kcalIT

Example 2

Convert 8000 joules to kilocalories.

E(kcal-IT) = 18000 J / 4186.8 = 1.910 kcalIT

Example 3

Convert 18000 joules to kilocalories.

E(kcal-IT) = 18000 J / 4186.8 = 4.299 kcalIT

How do you convert J to kcal mol?

In SI units, one kilocalorie per mole is equal to 4.184 kilojoules per mole (kJ/mol), which is approximately 6.9477×10−21 joules per molecule, or approximately 0.043 eV per molecule. At room temperature (25 °C, 77 °F, or 298.15 K) this is approximately equal to 1.688 units in the KT term of Boltzmann's equation. Read more
