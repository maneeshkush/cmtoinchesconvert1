How to convert BTU to kWh

How to convert BTU (IT) to kilowatt-hour:

1 BTUIT = 0.00029307107017 kWh

 

So the energy in kilowatt-hours E(kWh) is equal to 0.00029307107017 times the energy in BTUs E(BTU):

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × E(BTU)

 

Example 1

Convert 2000 BTU to kWh:

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × 2000 BTU = 0.586 kWh

Example 2

Convert 4000 BTU to kWh:

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × 4000 BTU = 1.172 kWh

Example 3

Convert 6000 BTU to kWh:

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × 6000 BTU = 1.758 kWh

Example 4

Convert 8000 BTU to kWh:

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × 8000 BTU = 2.344 kWh

Example 5

Convert 10000 BTU to kWh:

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × 10000 BTU = 2.930 kWh

Example 6

Convert 100000 BTU to kWh:

E(kWh) = 0.00029307107017 × 100000 BTU = 29.307 kWh

How do I calculate kWh of heater?

To calculate your heater's kWh, estimate the time it will take to use it and write down the wattage of the appliance.
Divide wattage by 1,000 to calculate kilowatts: 1500 watts by 1,000 = 1.5 kilowatts.
Multiply kilowatts by the hours of daily use: 1.5 kW X 2 hours = 3 kWh per day.
