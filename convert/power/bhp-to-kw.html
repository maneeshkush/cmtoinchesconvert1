How to convert BHP to kW

One mechanical brake horsepower is equal to 0.745699872 kilowatts:

1 hp = 745.699872 W = 1.491399744 kW

2 mechanical brake horsepower is equal to 0.745699872 kilowatts:

2 hp = 1.491399744 W = 1.491399744 kW

So the power conversion of BHP to kilowatts is given by:

P(kW) = 0.745699872 × P(hp)

Example 1

Convert 120 brake horsepower to kW:

P(kW) = 0.745699872 × 120hp = 89.48398464 kW

Example 2

Convert 140 brake horsepower to kW:

P(kW) = 0.745699872 × 140hp = 104.39798208 kW

Example 3

Convert 170 brake horsepower to kW:

P(kW) = 0.745699872 × 170hp = 126.76897824 kW

Example 4

Convert 420 brake horsepower to kW:

P(kW) = 0.745699872 × 420hp = 313.19394624 kW

Example 5

Convert 1120 brake horsepower to kW:

P(kW) = 0.745699872 × 1120hp = 835.18385664 kW
