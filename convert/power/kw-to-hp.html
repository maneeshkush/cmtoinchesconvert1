How to convert kilowatts to horsepower

Kilowatts to mechanic / hydraulic horsepower

1 mechanic or hydraulic horsepower is equal to [0.745699872] kilowatts.

1 hp(I) = 745.699872 W = 0.745699872 kW

So the power conversion of kilowatts to horsepower is given by

P(hp) = P(kW) / 0.745699872

Example 1

Convert 17kW to mechanic horsepower:

P(hp) = 17kW / 0.745699872 = 22.79737551 hp

Example 2

Convert 45kW to mechanic horsepower:

P(hp) = 45kW / 0.745699872 = 60.345993998 hp

Example 3

Convert 10kW to mechanic horsepower:

P(hp) = 10kW / 0.745699872 = 134.10220888 hp

 

Kilowatts to electrical horsepower

One electrical horsepower is equal to 0.746 kilowatts:

1 hp(E) = 746 W = 0.746 kW

So the power conversion of kilowatts to horsepower is given by:

P(hp) = P(kW) / 0.746

Example 1

Convert 17kW to electrical horsepower:

P(hp) = 17kW / 0.746 = 22.788203753 hp

Example 2

Convert 45kW to electrical horsepower:

P(hp) = 45kW / 0.746 = 60.321715818 hp

Example 3

Convert 100kW to electrical horsepower:

P(hp) = 100kW / 0.746 = 134.04825737 hp

 

Kilowatts to metric horsepower

1 metric horsepower is equal to [0.73549875] kilowatts.

1 hp(M) = 735.49875 W = 0.73549875 kW

So the power conversion of kilowatts to horsepower is given by.

P(hp) = P(kW) / 0.73549875

Example 1

Convert 17kW to metric horsepower:

P(hp) = 17kW / 0.73549875 = 23.113567494 hp

Example 2

Convert 45kW to metric horsepower:

P(hp) = 45kW / 0.73549875 = 61.182972779 hp

Example 3

Convert 100kW to metric horsepower:

P(hp) = 100kW / 0.73549875 = 135.96216173 hp
