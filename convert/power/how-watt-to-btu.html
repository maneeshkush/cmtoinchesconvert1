How to convert watts to BTU/hr

How to convert watts (W) to British Thermal Units per hour (BTU/hr).

One watt is equal to 3.412142 BTU per hour:

1 W = 3.412142 BTU/hr

Watts to BTU/hr conversion formula

The power in BTUIT per hour P(BTU/hr) is equal to 3.412142 times the power in watts P(W):

P(BTU/hr) = 3.412142 ⋅ P(W)

Example 1

Convert 1000W to BTU/hr:

P(BTU/hr) = 3.412142 ⋅ 1000W = 3412.14 BTU/hr

Example 2

Convert 2000W to BTU/hr:

P(BTU/hr) = 3.412142 ⋅ 2000W = 6824.28 BTU/hr

Example 3

Convert 4000W to BTU/hr:

P(BTU/hr) = 3.412142 ⋅ 4000W = 13648.56 BTU/hr

Example 4

Convert 6000W to BTU/hr:

P(BTU/hr) = 3.412142 ⋅ 6000W = 20472.84 BTU/hr

Example 5

Convert 8000W to BTU/hr:

P(BTU/hr) = 3.412142 ⋅ 8000W = 27297.13 BTU/hr
