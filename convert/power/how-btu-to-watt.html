How to convert BTU/hr to watts

How to convert BTU per hour (BTU/hr) to watts (W).

One BTU/hr is equal to 0.29307107 watts:

1 BTU/hr = 0.29307107 W

BTU/hr to watts conversion formula

The power in watts P(W) is equal to the power in BTUIT per hour P(BTU/hr) divided by 3.412142:

P(W) = P(BTU/hr) / 3.412142

Example 1

Convert 2000 BTU/hr to watts:

P(W) = 2000 BTU/hr / 3.412142 = 586.14214W

Example 2

Convert 4000 BTU/hr to watts:

P(W) = 4000 BTU/hr / 3.412142 = 1172.28428W

Example 3

Convert 6000 BTU/hr to watts:

P(W) = 6000 BTU/hr / 3.412142 = 1758.42642W

Example 4

Convert 8000 BTU/hr to watts:

P(W) = 8000 BTU/hr / 3.412142 = 2344.56856W

Example 5

Convert 10000 BTU/hr to watts:

P(W) = 10000 BTU/hr / 3.412142 = 2930.7107W

 

P(W) = 20000 BTU/hr / 3.412142 = 5861.42W
