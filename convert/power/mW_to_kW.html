How to convert milliwatts to kilowatts

So The power in kilowatts P(kW) is equal to the power in milliwatts P(mW) divided by one million.

P(kW) = P(mW) / 1000000

So

1mW = 0.000001kW

5mW = 0.000005kW

Example 1

Convert 200 milliwatts to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 200mW / 1000000 = 0.0002kW

Example 2

Convert 400 milliwatts to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 400mW / 1000000 = 0.0004kW

Example 3

Convert 3000 milliwatts to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 3000mW / 1000000 = 0.003kW

Example 4

Convert 5000 milliwatts to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 5000mW / 1000000 = 0.005kW

Example 5

Convert 7000 milliwatts to kilowatts:
