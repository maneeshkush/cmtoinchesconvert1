How to convert kW to BHP

1 mechanical brake horsepower is equal to [0.745699872] kilowatts.

1 hp = 745.699872 W = 0.745699872 kW

So the power conversion of kilowatts to BHP is given by.

P(hp) = P(kW) / 0.745699872

Example 1

Convert 10 kW to BHP:

P(hp) = 10 kW /  0.745699872 = 13.410 hp

Example 2

Convert 50 kW to BHP:

P(hp) = 50 kW /  0.745699872 = 67.051 hp

Example 3

Convert 200 kW to BHP:

P(hp) = 200 kW /  0.745699872 = 268.204 hp

Example 4

Convert 500 kW to BHP:

P(hp) = 500 kW /  0.745699872 = 670.511 hp

Example 5

Convert 1000 kW to BHP:

P(hp) = 1000 kW /  0.745699872 = 1341.022 hp
