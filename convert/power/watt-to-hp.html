How to convert watts to horsepower

Watts to mechanic / hydraulic horsepower

1 mechanic or hydraulic horsepower is equal to [745.699872] watts.

1 hp(I) = 745.699872 W

So the power conversion of watts to horsepower is given by.

P(hp) = P(W) / 745.699872

Example 1

Convert 17W to mechanic horsepower:

P(hp) = 17W / 745.699872 = 0.0227 hp

Example 2

Convert 20W to mechanic horsepower:

P(hp) = 20W / 745.699872 = 0.026820441777 hp

Example 3

Convert 30W to mechanic horsepower:

P(hp) = 30W / 745.699872 = 0.040230662665 hp
