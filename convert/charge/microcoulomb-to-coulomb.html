Microcoulombs to coulombs formula

The charge in coulombs Q(C) is equal to the charge in microcoulombs Q(μC) divided by 1000000:

Q(C) = Q(μC) / 1000000

Example 1

Convert 2 microcoulombs to coulombs:

Q(C) = 2μC / 1000000 = 0.000002C

Example 2

Convert 15 microcoulombs to coulombs:
