Ampere-hours to milliampere-hours formula

The charge in milliampere-hours Q(mAh) is equal to the charge in ampere-hours Q(Ah) times 1000:

Q(mAh) = Q(Ah) × 1000

Example 1

Convert 2 ampere-hours to milliampere-hours:

Q(mAh) = 2Ah × 1000 = 2000mAh

Example 2

Convert 5 ampere-hours to milliampere-hours:

Q(mAh) = 5Ah × 1000 = 5000mAh

Example 3

Convert 10 ampere-hours to milliampere-hours:

Q(mAh) = 10Ah × 1000 = 10000mAh

Example 4

Convert 50 ampere-hours to milliampere-hours:
