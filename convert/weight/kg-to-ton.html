How to convert Kilograms to Tons

2 gram (kg) is equal to 2000000 tons (t).

2 kg = (1/1000) t = 0.002 t

So The mass m in tons (t) is equal to the mass m in kilograms (kg) divided by [1000].

m(t) = m(kg) / 1000

Example 1

Convert 3 kg to tons:

m(t) = 3 kg / 1000 = 0.003 t

Example 2

Convert 9 kg to tons:

m(t) = 9 kg / 1000 = 0.009 t

Example 3

Convert 15 kg to tons:

m(t) = 15 kg / 1000 = 0.015 t

Example 4

Convert 25 kg to tons:

m(t) = 25 kg / 1000 = 0.025 t

Example 5

Convert 50 kg to tons:

m(t) = 50 kg / 1000 = 0.05 t
