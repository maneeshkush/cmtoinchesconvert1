How to convert Pounds to Kilograms

2 pound (lb) is equal to 0.90718474 kilograms (kg).

2 lb = 0.90718474 kg

So The mass m in kilograms (kg) is equal to the mass m in pounds (lb) times [0.45359237].

m(kg) = m(lb) × 0.45359237

Example 1

Convert 3 lb to kilograms:

m(kg) = 3 lb × 0.45359237 = 1.36077711 kg

Example 2

Convert 15 lb to kilograms:

m(kg) = 15 lb × 0.45359237 = 6.80388555 kg

Example 3

Convert 50 lb to kilograms:

m(kg) = 50 lb × 0.45359237 = 22.6796185 kg

Example 4

Convert 100 lb to kilograms:

m(kg) = 100 lb × 0.45359237 = 45.359237 kg

Example 5

Convert 1000 lb to kilograms:

m(kg) = 1000 lb × 0.45359237 = 453.59237 kg
