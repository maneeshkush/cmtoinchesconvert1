How to convert Kilograms to Grams

2 kilogram (kg) is equal to 2000 grams (g).

2 kg = 2000 g

8 kilogram (kg) is equal to 8000 grams (g).

8 kg = 8000 g

So mass m in grams (g) is equal to the mass m in kilograms (kg) times [1000].

m(g) = m(kg) × 1000

Example 1

Convert 3kg to grams:

m(g) = 3 kg × 1000 = 3000 g

Example 2

Convert 12kg to grams:

m(g) = 12 kg × 1000 = 12000 g

Example 3

Convert 15kg to grams:

m(g) = 15 kg × 1000 = 15000 g

Example 4

Convert 25kg to grams:

Example 5
