How to Convert Grams to Ounces

So 1 gram (g) is equal to 0.03527396195 ounces (oz).

1 g = 0.03527396195 oz

So The mass m in ounces (oz) is equal to the mass m in grams (g) divided by [28.34952].

m(oz) = m(g) / 28.34952

Example 1

Convert 4g to Ounces:

m(oz) = 4 g / 28.34952 = 0.1410958478 oz

Example 2

Convert 6g to Ounces:

m(oz) = 6 g / 28.34952 = 0.2116437717 oz

Example 3

Convert 50g to Ounces:

m(oz) = 50 g / 28.34952 = 1.7636980975 oz

Example 4

Convert 100g to Ounces:

m(oz) = 100 g / 28.34952 = 3.527396195 oz

Example 5

Convert 500g to Ounces:

m(oz) = 500 g / 28.34952 = 17.636980975 oz
