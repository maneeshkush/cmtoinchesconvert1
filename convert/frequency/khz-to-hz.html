How to convert kilohertz to Hz

1kHz = 1000Hz

2kHz = 2000Hz

or

1Hz = 0.001kHz

2Hz = 0.002kHz

Kilohertz to hertz formula

So frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to the frequency f in kilohertz (kHz) times 1000:

f(Hz) = f(kHz) × 1000

Example 1

Convert 2 kilohertz to hertz:

f(Hz) = 2kHz × 1000 = 2000Hz

Example 2

Convert 4 kilohertz to hertz:

f(Hz) = 24kHz × 1000 = 4000Hz

Example 3

Convert 9 kilohertz to hertz:

Example 4
