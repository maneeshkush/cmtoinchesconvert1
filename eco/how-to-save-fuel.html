How to save fuel

How to save fuel. How to reduce fuel consumption.

Reduce fuel consumption

  • Here are 50+ ways to save fuel and reduce fuel consumption:

    1. Keep your tires properly inflated. Underinflated tires can decrease fuel efficiency by up to 0.6% for every 1 PSI drop in pressure of all four tires.

    2. Use the correct oil. Using the manufacturer-recommended grade of oil can improve fuel efficiency by 1-2%.

    3. Remove excess weight from your vehicle. Every 100 pounds of unnecessary weight in your vehicle can reduce fuel efficiency by up to 2%.

    4. Avoid excessive idling. Idling your vehicle for more than a minute uses more fuel than restarting the engine.

    5. Use cruise control. Maintaining a steady speed on the highway can improve fuel efficiency by up to 15%.

    6. Avoid rapid acceleration and braking. Sudden acceleration and braking can decrease fuel efficiency by up to 33% in city driving.

    7. Use the highest appropriate gear. Shift to a higher gear as soon as possible when driving a manual transmission vehicle.

    8. Use overdrive gears. Overdrive gears can improve fuel efficiency on the highway by up to 10%.
