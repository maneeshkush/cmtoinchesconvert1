Fórmula de microcoulombs a coulombs

La càrrega en coulombs Q (C) és igual a la càrrega en microcoulombs Q (μC) dividida per 1000000:

Q(C) = Q(μC) / 1000000

What is the difference between μC and C?

The main difference between μC and C is the scale of the units. One microcoulomb is equal to 1/1,000,000 of a coulomb, or 0.000001 C. In other words, 1,000,000 μC is equal to 1 C. This means that μC is typically used to measure smaller values of electric charge, while C is used to measure larger values.

How do I convert μC to C?

Per convertir un valor en μC a C, simplement podeu dividir el valor en μC per 1.000.000. Per exemple, per convertir 500.000 μC a C, dividiríeu 500.000 per 1.000.000, la qual cosa us dóna 0,5 C.

Com puc convertir C a μC?

Per convertir un valor de C a μC, només podeu multiplicar el valor de C per 1.000.000. Per exemple, per convertir 2 C en μC, multiplicaríeu 2 per 1.000.000, la qual cosa us donarà 2.000.000 μC.
